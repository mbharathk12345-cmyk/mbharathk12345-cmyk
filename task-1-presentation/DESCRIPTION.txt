For this task, I created a digital literacy infographic using a simple design approach inspired by Canva-style layouts. The infographic presents key concepts of digital literacy in a clear and visually organized manner. It includes four main sections: what digital literacy is, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. Each section is placed in a separate colored box to improve readability and make the content visually appealing.

The design highlights important tools such as Google Classroom, Notion, Zoom, and Google Drive, which help students in learning and collaboration. It also emphasizes safe internet practices like using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links, along with basic email etiquette for professional communication.

One interesting aspect I found while creating this infographic was how important layout and color selection are in making information easy to understand. Initially, arranging the sections properly was challenging, but using a structured format helped in presenting the information effectively. Overall, this task helped me understand how to communicate important digital concepts in a simple and engaging visual format.
